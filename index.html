My Story

Creative Photography

Hi! My name is Hanna Daniels, and I'm the founder of Hanna Daniels Photography. I'm a North Carolina-based fashion and portrait photographer who specializes in making my clients feel comfortable and confident in the spotlight, regardless of their experiences in the fashion industry. I love being able to make people recognize the beauty that they already possess through the images I'm able to capture.

I've had the pleasure to spread my wings in the beauty industry by being on both sides of the camera! I've spent the last 4 years as a travelling makeup artist and had the opportunity to work on several professional projects with Goldwell Color as their official on-set makeup artist. I've also worked on a handful of theater productions and Indy films such as “Auto Repair”.

Since then, I've begun to focus solely on my photography business. I've jumped in head-first and worked on a variety of projects with several modeling agencies in the greater Raleigh area. My absolute favorite project to date is my collaboration with Vogue and Gucci Beauty on their Spring 2020 campaign: "Unconventional Beauty".

I've discovered a reckless passion for fashion photography and have hopes of travelling to New York and Los Angeles to shoot various fashion shows and eventually work on magazine spreads for Vogue, Times, Rolling Stone, and Bazaar Magazines.

By booking a shoot, not only are you supporting a local artist, but you're also making a contribution to help me realize my dreams and ambitions so I can chase my calling.

Come visit me at the premiere of Raw Artists in Raleigh, Jan 30, 2020 @7:00PM at The Ritz

I look forward to working with you!
